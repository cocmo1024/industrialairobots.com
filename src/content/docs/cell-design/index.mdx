# Cell Design

Cell design is where technical promise meets operational reality. Good cell design connects the robot, tooling, guarding, material flow, maintenance access, and operator interactions into something the plant can actually run.

## Cell design questions

<Steps>
	1. Where do parts enter, wait, transfer, and exit?
	2. How do guarding, access, and operator intervention affect the layout?
	3. What maintenance and recovery actions must happen without breaking the cell design?
	4. Which design choices protect throughput instead of only optimizing demo behavior?
</Steps>
