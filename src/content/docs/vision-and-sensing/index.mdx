## Vision review flow

<Steps>
	1. Identify what variability the robot must actually resolve.
	2. Decide whether sensing is needed for guidance, inspection, quality confirmation, or all three.
	3. Map how environment, lighting, cycle time, and maintenance influence the sensing stack.
	4. Compare the operational burden of perception against the value it creates.
</Steps>
