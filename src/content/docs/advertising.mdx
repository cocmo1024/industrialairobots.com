Industrial AI Robots may support advertising in the future, but the site is not intended to operate as a pay-to-rank directory or vendor placement board.

## Current position

If advertising is introduced, it should stay clearly separate from editorial conclusions and should not change page rankings, product inclusion, or coverage decisions.

## What advertising should not do

- It should not purchase favorable coverage.
- It should not determine which vendor or product families receive pages.
- It should not weaken the editorial standard for depth, sourcing, or usefulness.

## Separation from editorial judgment

Commercial placements should remain visibly labeled and separate from analysis, recommendations, and comparisons.
