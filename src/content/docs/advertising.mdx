Industrial AI Robots reserves commercial positions in the layout so future monetization can be introduced without redesigning the site or blurring editorial boundaries.

## Reserved inventory model

Advertising is organized by page type, not by ad hoc placement decisions on individual pages. This makes it easier to scale a large reference library while keeping layouts predictable.

## Current status

Some pages show clearly labeled reserved commercial placements. Those placeholders do not necessarily indicate that paid campaigns are currently live.

## Large-site ad management

The site is designed to manage inventory by content profile:

- foundation pages carry the lightest commercial load;
- application and case-study pages support higher-intent placements;
- deployment and ROI pages are reserved for the most commercially relevant inventory.

This route-based approach keeps ad operations manageable as the number of pages, sections, and categories grows.

## Separation from editorial judgment

Commercial placements should remain visibly labeled and separate from analysis, recommendations, and comparisons.
