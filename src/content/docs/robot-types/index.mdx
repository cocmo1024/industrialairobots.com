## Selection questions

<Steps>
	1. What payload, reach, repeatability, and speed are required?
	2. How much variability exists in part presentation, changeover, or human proximity?
	3. What guarding or collaborative design constraints are realistic in this environment?
	4. Which class minimizes total cell complexity instead of only looking attractive in demos?
</Steps>
