Industrial AI Robots is an English-language reference site focused on the practical side of industrial robotics, machine vision, and AI-assisted automation systems.

## Scope

- Robotics applications organized by industrial job type
- Vision, sensing, and cell-level systems that affect deployment success
- Deployment, scaling, and ROI-oriented planning guidance

## Audience

This site is intended for manufacturers, automation teams, engineers, consultants, integrators, and technical decision-makers evaluating robotic systems in production environments.

## Editorial approach

Coverage is meant to mature into a durable application and deployment library. The emphasis is on operational relevance, decision support, and long-term usefulness rather than hype-driven commentary.
