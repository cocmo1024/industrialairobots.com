The **Industrial AI Robots Review Desk** exists to keep high-intent pages from reading like polished robotics marketing.

## What gets reviewed

The review desk focuses on whether a page:

- reflects a credible manufacturing constraint,
- makes its rollout assumptions explicit,
- distinguishes pilot success from scale-up readiness,
- and gives readers enough detail to understand where the cell can actually break.

## What review is trying to stop

The review desk is trying to prevent pages that:

- confuse a successful demo with a robust production cell,
- hide service and maintenance burden behind throughput claims,
- or reuse generic automation language without enough factory context.

## When pages should be revisited

Pages are candidates for re-review when:

- a robot family changes positioning enough to affect fit,
- a case-study cluster starts overlapping too heavily,
- or a page still answers the topic but no longer answers it in the most useful way.
