# Change Management and Maintenance Readiness

Many robotics projects fail after installation, not because the robot cannot move, but because the organization is not ready to support the cell when variation, faults, and staffing reality appear.

## Readiness questions

- Who owns first-response troubleshooting on each shift?
- How are restart, recovery, and escalation procedures documented?
- Are maintenance teams prepared for the actual mix of robot, vision, tooling, and safety systems?

## Why this matters financially

Weak support ownership turns promising ROI models into unstable operations. Maintenance readiness is often one of the hidden variables that separates a showcase pilot from a scalable automation program.
