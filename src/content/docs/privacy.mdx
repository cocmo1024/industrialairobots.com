Industrial AI Robots is designed as a reference site. Limited technical information may be processed to keep the site available, secure it against abuse, and understand which robotics topics are useful to readers.

## Information that may be collected

- Basic server or browser metadata
- Aggregate analytics related to page usage and navigation
- Information voluntarily submitted through email communication

## How information may be used

- Operate, secure, and improve the site
- Understand which applications, robot types, and deployment topics are most useful to readers
- Respond to editorial, correction, or commercial communication

## Third-party services

Hosting, analytics, or infrastructure vendors may process limited technical information as part of normal site delivery. Their own privacy practices may also apply.

## Sensitive information

This site is not intended for submitting sensitive personal, regulatory, plant-security, or confidential project information. Do not send protected operational data through routine contact channels.
